The Mind Connection: A New Path to Healing

At The Mind Connection, we take a holistic approach to mental health, addressing both brain and body, to help our clients feel and function better. As holistic providers, we prioritize natural and safe treatment methods over pharmaceuticals. We are concerned that our population has never been more medicated, but people have also never been sicker.

Obviously, medications aren't fixing the problem. Our solutions offer non-invasive, drug free brain healing as an alternative to medications that do not address the underlying cause of mental, physical or emotional issues. Let us help heal your brain and find balance so that you can feel and function better WITHOUT medications and their unwanted side effects.

Janelle Martin is a married mother of two children from Magnolia, Texas. She has a Bachelor's degree in Biology and Chemistry from the University of Northern Iowa, an Accounting degree from LoneStar College and a Masters degree in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Lamar University. She is a Licensed Chemical Dependency Counselor (LCDC), Licensed Professional Counselor Associate (LPC-A), Dr. Amen Certified Brain Health Professional, and certified in Brainspotting.

Janelle was formally trained as an IASIS Certified Provider (ICP) in August, 2019. Through Janelle's extensive experience using IASIS MicroCurrent Neurofeedback, she has witnessed many incredible transformations in her clients, especially when conducted alongside traditional therapy. She strives to keep IASIS MCN available and affordable to anyone wanting to improve their quality of life.

Since about 2008, Janelle began her journey into natural ways of healing and supporting the body. This quest for knowledge and a non-toxic way of living continues today, and is one of the reasons The Mind Connection was born. Utilizing IASIS MicroCurrent Neurofeedback, she witnessed clients no longer needing medications and thriving in their daily lives again. Consequently, she shares her research and healthy products in her office, encouraging all to consider alternative options for supporting brain and body health.

Janelle is very excited to be serving her community, advocating for natural approaches to mental health through brain and body healing. She offers counseling, Brainspotting and IASIS MicroCurrent Neurofeedback, along with natural supplements and products that encourage improved health and wellness, both physically and mentally. Additionally, she facilitates weekly PAL (Parents of Addicted Loved Ones) meetings and sponsors other parents as part of her personal 12-step recovery program.

Regan Steinert was born in Houston, Texas. Since the age of 3, she lived with her mother, stepfather and her older brother in Montgomery, TX. At the age of 17, she started her journey in recovery by getting sober at the Pathway Program, an alternative peer group (APG) in Arizona. Since being committed to her own recovery as a teenager, Regan has taken great strides in helping those in her community, and it has been her mission to share her story to help as many people she can. Regan has spoken on multiple podcasts such as ‘Let’s Talk Recovery Houston’, Mosaics of Mercy’s ‘Hope Pieced Together’, and Melissa Radke’s ‘Ordinary People, Ordinary Things’, as well as others.

She became a certified Recovery Coach in 2019, has experience in rehabilitation detox centers, alternative peer groups, as well as recovery centers. She is also a Licensed Chemical Dependency Counselor Intern (LCDC-I), IASIS Certified Provider (ICP) of IASIS MicroCurrent Neurofeedback and trained in Brainspotting therapy. Her specialty is working with teens and young adults who are dealing with substance use issues themselves or have a loved one who is affected by substance use. She is simultaneously continuing her education toward her plan to become a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC).
