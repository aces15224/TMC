What is IASIS MicroCurrent Neurofeedback?

MicroCurrent Neurofeedback (MCN) is a non-invasive, non-pharmaceutical treatment safe for people of all ages! MCN provides a healthy option of treatment for people suffering from a wide array of issues. Studies and reports have shown enduring and sustainable change after a short number of treatments.

How does Neurofeedback therapy work?

With repeated exposure to stress, illness or damage to brain tissue, the central nervous system (CNS) becomes unbalanced. This imbalance leads to a multitude of impairments in health and overall quality of life. Through the use of carefully calibrated and controlled electrical signals, or microcurrent, IASIS neurofeedback speaks to the brain in a language it can understand, "biofeedback to the brain", and gently nudges the CNS toward balance (homeostasis).

IASIS MCN, unlike medications, treats the brain as a whole, guiding any neural pathways that get stuck, back to optimal health and performance. Just like rebooting your computer at home, the process of MCN allows for the brain and nervous system to rebalance and regulate itself. During the treatment MCN is imperceptible to the client and completely painless, almost like a spa for your brain.

Our experience shows that more than 85% of our clients observe a noticeable positive response in one to three sessions. Reported changes are generally enduring and sustainable.
