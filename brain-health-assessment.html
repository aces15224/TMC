What is Creyos Brain Health Assessment Testing?

Creyos Health is an online brain health assessment service that accurately measures core elements of your cognitive function, including memory, attention, reasoning, and verbal abilities. We use these measures to assess, monitor and manage core areas of cognition that are key to your health and wellness.

✓

Convenient Creyos Brain Health Assessments are web-based and flexible. Assessments can be completed on desktop, laptop and tablet devices, no pen and paper or special hardware required

✓

Engaging Creyos Brain Health Assessment tasks take only 1.5 - 3 minutes to complete, and are highly engaging, enjoyable and unintimidating. Plus, interactive and repeatable task tutorials will ensure you get up to speed on the task instructions quickly

✓

Actionable Creyos Brain Health Assessment allows you to quantify the core elements of cognition and track your cognitive trends over time, validating that treatment or wellness plans are having the desired effects, or allowing you to detect episodic changes to your cognition
